The New Delhi Municipal Council (NDMC) is committed to providing efficient, transparent, and citizen-centric governance to the heart of the national capital. With a strong focus on sustainability, digital transformation, and public service excellence, NDMC continues to innovate in urban management through smart city projects, green infrastructure, and modernized civic services. From ensuring clean streets and well-maintained parks to promoting e-governance and energy-efficient solutions, NDMC is setting new benchmarks in urban administration and community engagement. Together, we are building a cleaner, smarter, and more responsive Delhi for the future.

About Us

New Delhi Municipal Council

The New Delhi Municipal Council (NDMC) is the municipal government of the city of New Delhi, India, and was formed under the NDMC Act 1994. It is responsible for an area of 42.7 kmÂ², that includes Lutyen's Delhi and important areas of Central Delhi.

Our mission is to provide efficient and transparent civic services to the citizens of New Delhi. We strive to maintain the highest standards of urban planning, sanitation, public health, and infrastructure development.

The NDMC is committed to sustainable development and environmental conservation, ensuring that New Delhi remains a green and clean capital city.
